ADVENTURERS HEAD FOR THE GRAND COURSE

The Grand Course is a beautiful hidden paradise surrounded by the fiercest and most uncharted waters in all of the world. From unpredictable tropical storms to undiscovered animals, The Grand Course is a destination for the worlds bravest adventurers. Adventurers are sailing off to discover what lies in The Grand Course. Some adventurers seek exotic plants for new medical advancements. Others are interested in the numerous islands full of undiscovered species. The Grand Course has become the most uncharted place in the world. Adventurers all across the globe are sailing to see for themselves what lies in The Grand Course.

CAPTAIN CROW STILL ON THE LOOSE

Warning from the Marines of The World Government:
The pirate and legendary warlord Captain Crow is on the loose. He was last seen heading towards the Grand Course. This pirate is extremely dangerous, please take caution if you happen to cross paths with this criminal. It is likely that Crow is after all the untapped resources and land located in the treacherous waters of the Grand Course. Crow and many other infamous pirates set sail to The Grand Course for the beginning of this new era.

MARINE ADMIRAL DRAKE SEEKS JUSTICE

With countless pirates sailing for the Grand Course there is concern that this hidden paradise could turn into a battle ground for all types of criminals. Marine Admiral Drake is leading the first fleet of ships to the Grand Course to ensure the area remains unharmed. Drake is leading the governments efforts to try and use the secure the land for government purposes. Many citizens arent happy with the response from the world government saying, "The Grand Course is an untapped paradise to house new navy bases." And with Admiral Drake's strength and fire abilities,he is tasked with taking on any threats along the way to concurring The Grand Course.
